About me

Hi, there! I'm a third-year Ph.D. student of High Performance Robotics (HiPeR) Lab @ UC Berkeley under supervision of Prof. Mark W. Mueller with both MechE Department and Berkeley AI Research (BAIR) Lab. Before this, I got my Bachelor's degree in Automotive Engineering of Tongji University in 2023.


My current and on-going research interest lies on Reinforcement Learning, Perception and Mechanical Design of Aerial Robot and Manipulation. Please contact me if you are interested in my research and I'm open to any academic collaboration!
