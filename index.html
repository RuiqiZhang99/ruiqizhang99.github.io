About me

Hi, there! I'm the 2nd-year Ph.D. student of High Performance Robotics (HiPeR) Lab @ UC Berkeley under supervision of Prof. Mark W. Mueller with both MechE Department and Berkeley AI Research (BAIR) Lab.


Before this, I got my Bachelor's degree in Automotive Engineering, Tongji University. I'm also a long-term researcher of Tongji Robotics & Embodied AI Lab supervised by Prof. Guang Chen. My research mainly focuses on Robotics, Reinforcement Learning and Computer Vision. I also collaborated with with Prof. Lin Shao and Dr. Xiao Ma at the School of Computing of National University of Singapore in summer of 2022. My former research (during undergraduate) mainly focused on Reinforcement Learning for motion planning in Autonomous Driving.

As a member of HiPeR Lab, my current and on-going research interest lies on Reinforcement Learning, Robotics, Control and Multi-Agent System. Specifically, I'm trying to use Deep Reinforcement Learning to solve complex model like aerodynamic effect in UAV control, and advanced design of the multimodal robots.
